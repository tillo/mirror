mirror.mdapi.ch

About

HTTP(S), FTP(S) and rsync services are anonymous, unmetered and offered for free.
Our infrastructure is private and housed at MDAPI in Echallens, Switzerland.

This is a personal project, please do not abuse it.
Contact Martino Dell'Ambrogio <tillo@tillo.ch> for any question or request.

Content

Here you can find a current mirror of the following projects:
  • blackarch: BlackArch Linux, download and Pacman repositories.
  • gentoo-portage: Gentoo Linux, Portage tree.
At the following locations (recommended secure protocols):
  • https://mirror.mdapi.ch/ftp/<project>
  • ftpes://mirror.mdapi.ch/<project>
And at the following locations (cleartext protocols):
  • rsync://mirror.mdapi.ch/<project>
  • http://mirror.mdapi.ch/ftp/<project>
  • ftp://mirror.mdapi.ch/<project>
